Executive Profile: Mr. Mohammad Munaf

Cybersecurity & Cloud Infrastructure Strategist | Chair at PASHA & FPCCI | National IT Advocate | CEC Member, PASHA

Mr. Mohammad Munaf is a recognized leader in cloud security, cybersecurity governance, and resilient digital infrastructure with 25+ years of experience advising banks, governments, and global enterprises. As Founder of Server4Sale and BlockDOS, he has delivered enterprise-grade DDoS protection, secure multi-cloud architectures, and digital risk consulting across BFSI, telecom, and public sectors.

My Core Area of expertise:

  • • National Cybersecurity Frameworks
  • • Cybercrime Strategy & Infrastructure Defense
  • • Digital Resilience for Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs)
  • • Technology Career and Startup Mentorship

Whether you represent a policy-making body, enterprise, academic institution, or emerging tech startup, I welcome opportunities for collaboration, advisory engagements, and knowledge-sharing sessions. Together, we can build a safer, more resilient digital Pakistan.
